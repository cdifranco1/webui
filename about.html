About Weightlifting Journal

Weightlifting Journal is an application created during Lambda build week by current Lambda School students through cross-functional collaboration.

We developed the Weightlifting Journal as a full-functioning application for tracking fitness sets and exercises to help users achieve their fitness goals. The application is flexible and allows users to customize their interface for personalized workouts.
